Potential growth opportunities as a computer engineer at barclays:

  • Software Engineer/Developer:
    • Software engineers develop and maintain software applications and systems used by Barclays, including banking platforms, trading systems, risk management tools, and customer-facing applications.
    • Growth opportunities: Advancement to senior software engineer roles, leadership positions within development teams, specialization in specific technologies or domains, involvement in high-impact projects.
    • Programming Languages: Java, Python, C++, C#
    • Frameworks and Libraries: Spring Framework, Angular, React, Node.js
    • Database Technologies: Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, MongoDB
    • DevOps and Automation: Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, GitLab

  • DevOps Engineer:
    • DevOps engineers focus on integrating development and operations processes, enabling the continuous integration, delivery, and deployment of software systems.
    • Growth opportunities: Taking on more complex DevOps initiatives, leading infrastructure automation projects, driving the adoption of cloud technologies, collaborating with cross-functional teams.
    • Infrastructure Automation: Ansible, Terraform
    • Frameworks and Libraries: Spring Framework, Angular, React, Node.js
    • Containerization and Orchestration: Docker, Kubernetes
    • CI/CD Tools: Jenkins, Gitlab
    • Cloud Technologies: AWS, Azure, GCP

  • Data Engineer:
    • Data engineers design and maintain data pipelines, databases, and data infrastructure to enable data-driven decision-making and analytics within Barclays.
    • Growth opportunities: Specializing in big data technologies, advancing to senior data engineer roles, working on large-scale data projects, contributing to data governance and data quality initiatives.
    • Data Processing: Apache Hadoop, Apache Spark
    • Data storage: Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, MongoDB, Apache Kafka
    • Big data and Analytics: Apache Hive, Elasticsearch, Kibana

  • Cybersecurity Engineer:
    • Cybersecurity engineers ensure the security and protection of Barclays' systems and data, developing and implementing security measures, conducting vulnerability assessments, and responding to security incidents.
    • Growth opportunities: Specializing in specific areas of cybersecurity, advancing to senior cybersecurity roles, leading security initiatives, staying updated with emerging threats and technologies.
    • Security Tools: Splunk, SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) Systems
    • Vulnerability Assessment: Nessus, Qualys
    • Security Monitoring: IDS/IPS (Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems), Firewall Technologies

  • Infrastructure Engineer:
    • Infrastructure engineers focus on designing, implementing, and maintaining the technology infrastructure that supports Barclays' operations, including networks, servers, storage, and virtualization.
    • Growth opportunities: Expertise in cloud computing and hybrid infrastructures, advancing to senior infrastructure engineer roles, involvement in infrastructure modernization projects, staying updated with emerging technologies.
    • Cloud Platforms: AWS, Azure, GCP
    • Networking: Cisco, Juniper, Palo Alto Networks
    • Virtualization: VMware, Hyper-V
    • Monitoring and Management: Nagios, Zabbix, Puppet

  • Product Owner/Manager:
    • Product owners/managers work closely with stakeholders to define product requirements, prioritize features, and ensure the successful delivery of technology products and solutions within Barclays.
    • Growth opportunities: Leading product development initiatives, driving product strategy and roadmap, taking on managerial positions, expanding product management responsibilities across multiple teams.
    • Collaboration and Project Management: JIRA, Confluence
    • Agile Methodologies: Scrum, Kanban
    • Product Analytics: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
    • User Research and Testing: Usability Testing, A/B Testing Tools
